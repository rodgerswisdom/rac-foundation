About Us

Raise a Child Foundation (RAC) is a Kenyan non-governmental organization focused on assisting needy children. Our mission is to empower children for a brighter tomorrow by providing essential resources and support.

Our Mission

To provide essential resources and support to needy children, ensuring they have the opportunity to succeed and thrive.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to education, healthcare, and the support they need to reach their full potential.

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Empowerment
  • Inclusivity

Our Journey

2010 - Foundation Established

Raise a Child Foundation was established with the goal of supporting needy children in Kenya.

2012 - First Major Donation Drive

We organized our first major donation drive, providing school supplies to over 500 children.

2015 - Education Retreats

We began organizing motivational education retreats to inspire and empower children.

Our Objectives

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

We promote menstrual hygiene awareness to ensure girls can attend school without interruption.

Donations for School Essentials

We collect and distribute school supplies to children in need, ensuring they have the tools to succeed.

Motivational Education Retreats

We organize retreats to inspire and motivate children to pursue their education and dreams.

Orphanage Visits and Equal Education Opportunities

We visit orphanages and advocate for equal education opportunities for all children.
